Introduction

At Methologik Pvt Ltd, we began our journey in 2017 with a vision to redefine design and development. Starting as a modest department, we have evolved into a powerhouse of technology and custom software development, serving both local and international clients with distinction.

By the end of 2020, we proudly launched our first Saudi brand Imdady, now trusted by 100+ satisfied customers. Over the years, Methologik has expanded its expertise into custom solutions, product development, project management, and design thinking, all underpinned by strong UX/UI design principles.

Our portfolio includes successful projects across e-commerce, healthcare, fintech, and transportation, and we continue to grow our footprint in new industries. We also offer outsourcing services, providing on-demand development teams to meet specific client goals efficiently.

A major milestone was our WASl integration with a transportation application in Saudi Arabia a testament to our capability in delivering complex, mission-critical systems.
