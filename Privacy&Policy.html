Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Methologik Pvt. Ltd. (“we”, “our”, “us”), your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with our website or use our services. We are committed to transparency, compliance, and safeguarding the personal information of all our users and clients.

2. Scope of Policy

This policy applies to:

  • Visitors of www.methologik.com
  • Clients and partners interacting with our products or services
  • Individuals submitting inquiries, proposals, or collaborating on projects

3. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We may collect the following data when you fill out a form, contact us, or use our services:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Company Name
  • Design or development brief
b. Technical & Usage Data
  • IP Address
  • Device Type & Browser
  • Pages visited, time spent, and click behavior
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (with consent where required)
c. Project-Related Data
  • Scope of work, project details, preferences, and timelines

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Provide and manage services
  • Respond to inquiries and proposals
  • Communicate project updates and offers (with your consent)
  • Analyze website performance and user behavior
  • Enhance user experience and website security

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Our data processing is based on:

  • Your Consent: when you submit information or subscribe to updates
  • Contractual Necessity: to fulfill a service or request
  • Legitimate Interests: such as improving services, analytics, or security

6. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Trusted third-party providers: such as hosting platforms, analytics tools, CRM services
  • Legal authorities: if required under applicable laws or lawful requests

All third parties are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Understand visitor preferences
  • Improve performance and speed
  • Collect anonymized traffic data

You can control cookie settings through your browser at any time.

8. Data Storage & Security

We follow industry-standard practices to protect your information:

  • Encrypted connections (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure servers and cloud infrastructure
  • Restricted access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular audits and security updates

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for:

  • Ongoing projects or services
  • Legal or regulatory compliance
  • Internal analysis (anonymized where possible)

Afterward, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

10. Your Rights

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent for marketing or communication
  • Restrict or object to processing in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@methologik.com

11. International Data Transfers

If your data is transferred outside of Pakistan (e.g. to international cloud providers), we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or data transfer agreements.

12. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If such data is identified, it will be promptly deleted.

13. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. All changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. If changes are significant, you will be notified via our website or direct communication.
